The templates can be installed in several ways. If you are on Ubuntu or another Debian-based distro there is an installer for all the templates with step by step instructions [here](https://github.com/dohliam/libreoffice-impress-templates/wiki/Ubuntu-Debian-Install-Guide). However, they can also be installed one by one through Impress.

To install the templates individually, follow the instructions below.

## LibreOffice 4

[](#libreoffice-4)

1. In Impress, click on the File Menu, then Templates, then select *Manage*. (Or press `Ctrl+Shift+N`) to enter the template manager.)
2. Click on *My Templates* (it should be the first box on the top left).
3. Now there should be a small button labelled *Import* at the top left corner of the window. Click on it.
4. Browse to the .otp file you would like to install and click *Open*.
5. Your presentation template should appear in the My Templates folder for future use. (If you don't see it, it may help to exit the Template Manager and open it again.)
